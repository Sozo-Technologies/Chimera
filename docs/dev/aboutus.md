## About Us

The **Chimera Project Team** is a group of student developers working together to build an AI system that translates hand signs into readable text in real time.

We are passionate about exploring **computer vision, artificial intelligence, and human-computer interaction**, while also improving our skills in software development and teamwork.

This project is part of our academic journey, where we aim to combine learning with real-world application. Each member contributes ideas, code, testing, and documentation to help the system grow.

---

### Our Goal

Our goal is to create a system that:

* Helps bridge communication through hand gestures
* Improves accessibility for sign-based communication
* Demonstrates practical use of AI in real-time systems
* Encourages collaboration and learning among students

---

### What We Believe

We believe that:

* Learning happens best through building real projects
* Every contributor has something valuable to offer
* Technology should help people communicate better
* Open collaboration leads to better innovation
